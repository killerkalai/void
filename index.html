why void exists

we built this because
no one should carry it alone

Most apps want you to share, post, react, perform. void is the opposite. It started with a simple truth — sometimes you don't need advice. You don't need likes. You don't need someone to fix you. You just need to say it. Out loud. Without fear. Without judgment. Without it following you.

We built void for the 3am thoughts. For the rage you can't show at work. For the grief you're tired of explaining. For every feeling that has nowhere to go. This is that place.
